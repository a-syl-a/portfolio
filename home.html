About Me
I am a web developer and a self-taught artist, my pen name is Asyla. I began learning design and art at the age of 14. Over time, I started freelancing, working on commissioned projects
for both local and international clients, specializing in portrait art and book covers.
I pursued web development because I already had a strong foundation in design and wanted to acquire a practical and essential skill for today’s digital age.
Web development proved to be the perfect choice.
